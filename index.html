As a Network Analyst at Stefanini Brasil, I provide network monitoring, troubleshooting, and configuration services for a large outsourcing contract with VALE S.A, a global mining company.

I work with devices from leading brands such as Cisco, Huawei, Datacom, and BlueCoat, and I have expertise in routing and switching protocols, firewall management, identity services, and proxy solutions.

I have also gained experience in service configuration of triple play (internet, IPTV, and VOIP) with a focus on ISP devices, as I previously worked as a Network Analyst and a Network Technician at Dinâmica Telecomunicações, a regional telecommunications company.

There, I was responsible for activating, configuring, and deactivating the network services for corporate and ISP customers, as well as analyzing and projecting improvements for the network infrastructure and backbone.

I am currently pursuing a degree in Computer Systems Networking and Telecommunications at UNOPAR, and I hold two certifications from MikroTik: MTCNA and MTCRE. I am also fluent in English, which I've improved during a 30-day exchange program in Manchester, England, in 2022 by EF - Education First School. I am motivated by learning new technologies, solving complex problems, and delivering high-quality network solutions.
